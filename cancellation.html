Cancellation Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

At Luxury Hotel Rwanda, we understand that travel plans can change unexpectedly. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair and transparent while ensuring we can provide the best service to all our guests. Please read this policy carefully before making your reservation.

Standard Cancellation Policy

More than 24 hours before check-in

Free Cancellation

Full refund of your deposit or payment

24 hours or less before check-in

One Night Charge

Charged for one night's stay

No-show

Full Charge

Charged for the entire reservation

Special Rate Cancellation Policies

Advance Purchase Rate

  • Non-refundable and non-changeable
  • Full payment required at time of booking
  • No refunds for cancellations or modifications
  • Best available rate with maximum savings

Flexible Rate

  • Free cancellation up to 24 hours before check-in
  • Modifications allowed subject to availability
  • Deposit required at time of booking
  • Balance due upon arrival

Package Deals

  • Cancellation policies vary by package
  • Please check specific package terms
  • May include non-refundable components
  • Changes subject to availability and fees

Refund Processing

  • Refunds will be processed within 5-10 business days
  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method
  • Bank processing times may vary depending on your financial institution
  • You will receive an email confirmation when the refund is processed
  • For credit card payments, the refund will appear on your next billing statement

Modification Policy

  • Date changes are subject to availability and rate differences
  • Room type changes may result in rate adjustments
  • Modifications within 24 hours of check-in may incur fees
  • Advance purchase rates cannot be modified
  • Contact us directly for modification requests

Group Bookings (10+ Rooms)

  • Different cancellation policies apply to group bookings
  • Deposit of 50% required at time of booking
  • Full payment due 30 days before arrival
  • Cancellation fees apply as follows:
    • 30+ days: 25% of total booking value
    • 15-29 days: 50% of total booking value
    • 7-14 days: 75% of total booking value
    • Less than 7 days: 100% of total booking value

Force Majeure and Exceptional Circumstances

In cases of force majeure events (natural disasters, government restrictions, health emergencies, etc.), we may offer more flexible cancellation options. These will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis and communicated to affected guests.

Travel Insurance

We strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance to protect against unexpected cancellations, medical emergencies, and other travel-related issues. Travel insurance can provide coverage for non-refundable expenses and offer peace of mind during your travels.
